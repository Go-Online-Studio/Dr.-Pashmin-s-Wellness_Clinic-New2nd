⚠️ Safety & Disclaimer

IV therapy is generally safe when administered by qualified professionals, but there are important considerations to be aware of:

  • Not suitable for everyone — a medical assessment is required before treatment
  • Requires a health screening and blood work review before first session
  • Possible side effects: dizziness, low BP, electrolyte imbalance in rare cases
  • Evidence for many wellness claims is still limited; treatments should be used as supportive care, not a cure
  • IV therapy is a supportive wellness tool — not a replacement for prescribed medications or medical treatment
